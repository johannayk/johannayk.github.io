101 Essays That Will
Change The Way You Think

written by Brianna West

a book that will surely shift your perspective on everyday life

Have you felt down? Confused? Stressed?
don't worry, we have all been there. It's a normal part of the human experience!
but that should not let you down at the end of the day. Brianna West uses her book
"101 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think" to both remind you of the normalness
of being human but also show you there are ways out of it through the use of your own
mind and perspectives. She shows that there are ways out of constantly being consumed
by your own thoughts and outer surrounding. Sometimes as humans we become so fixated
on the secular world, we neglect the most important thing in life. And that is ourselves.
With her advice and must needed transparency this book is truly going to help you understand
how to utilize your inner thoughts and strengths to not only be your most productive self, but the happiest.
